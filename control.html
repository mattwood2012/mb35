Moving Beyond 3.5
Playing in Control - The Six C's
The key concept to take away from the Moving Beyond 3.5 clinics is that of playing in control. A number of mental approaches to help you do this include:
  • Calm – mental strength, staying present, smiling, and replacing negative thoughts with awareness of your body, paddle, and opponents.
  • Concentration – narrow, external focus during points; wide, internal focus between points.
  • Consistent – choose shots within your capabilities and master a smaller set of reliable shots.
  • Confidence – as consistency improves, confidence naturally grows until misses feel surprising.
  • Calibrated – use warmup to dial in power, depth, and margin from the lines.
  • Coach – be your own coach after each rally and look into volunteering to coach others (but do not coach your partner in a game!)
