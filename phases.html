Five Phases in a Point - The Five P's

A rally can be thought of in terms of five phases or parts. These repeat until a player either makes an error or puts the ball away. These phases are:

  • Prepare – paddle up at around chest high, pointing roughly at the person hitting the ball and your body facing the ball (the Ready position)
  • Predict – watch the ball leave your opponent's paddle to read direction and speed
  • Plan – decide where and how to hit the ball
  • Position – get your body, feet, and paddle into a good position for your planned shot
  • Play – execute the shot with appropriate follow‑through

Then loop back to Prepare.
