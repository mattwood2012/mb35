Five Phases in a Point - The Five P's
A rally can be thought of in terms of five phases or parts. These repeat until a player either makes an error or puts the ball away. These phases are:
  • Prepare - paddle up at around chest high, pointing roughly at the person hitting the ball and your body facing the ball - a.k.a. the Ready position
  • Predict - give yourself time to react by watching the ball leave your opponent's paddle and see the balls direction and speed
  • Plan - decide on where and how to hit the ball
  • Position - get your body, feet, and paddle in a good position to play your planned shot
  • Play - hit the shot you have planned keeping your follow through appropriate to where on the court you hit the shot from

Then loop back to "Prepare"
